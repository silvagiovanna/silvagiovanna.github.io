Well, me!

Hello!
I'm Giovanna. 16 years and aries is my sign in case you need to know (sorry for that). I'm christian, and that's an important thing in my life, once its principles -- as love, faith, heaven's life and etc. -- are my whole life guidings. I go to church twice in a week.
Besides that, I love learn new things. Looooo-o-o-ve. This vacation, I've learned the main theme of my next post: programming. No spoilers! (click here to see)
Well, I think that a list could serve us very well.

  • Blogging: since 2010, I've made a thousand blogs and I've learned s much! Styling, writing, how to put images and fix errors inside the code. That was the zero step.
  • English: classic. I would watch videos and search every meaning. Today, I have a quilometrical personal dictionary.
  • French: I've choosed to love you! Around 2017, I started to learn french in Duolingo. That helped me build my how-to-learn system, so I have already a base to progress in any language. A thousand apps and "French Weeks" until comprehend fully what Cyprien (youtubeur) and Louane (chanteuse) say.
  • Knitting: I've started this year and it's amazing! In the first tries, there was only a few knots and a lot of chaotic wool. Today, I've already made ecobags, scarfs and doll's clothes (my little sister loves them).

All about The Big Bang Theory

As Detox says (with all wisdom in the world), I've had it! So, shut up everybody and let me enjoy this amazing TV-show.
You all should watch, really.
Anyway, I'm gonna spend some time with my people: Howard, Leonard, Raj, and Sheldon.
